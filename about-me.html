Michael Suggs

Welcome to my life. I am a proud father to a 4-month old little boy who has stolen my heart. When hearing about his arrival to our world, I decided that I needed to live the life I would want him to live. With that motivation and the unfortunately fortunate circumstances of being laid off of work due to COVID-19, I went on a search to find what brings me joy. I stumbled across a free coding camp and the rest is history. For the past year I have known that coding is my passion and have enjoyed the process of learning and creating new things. I am now in Springboard Software Engineering full-stack course and am excited to make this new found love my career.
